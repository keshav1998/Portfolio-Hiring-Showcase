This project provides a entralling way to generate matching score using GPT-4 and Chain of though prompting, with a RLHF pipeline that constantly helps in providign feedback from experts that helps in tuning the bot recursively and makes the scores that are being generated more dynamic and useful. We use giskard to eliminate risk of bias, hallucinations and performance issues.
